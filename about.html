About The World of Bicycles

The World of Bicycles was founded in 2020 by Alex Reynolds, a lifelong cyclist who has logged over 80,000 miles across road, mountain, and commuter bikes. What started as a personal blog documenting weekend rides and maintenance experiments has grown into a trusted resource for tens of thousands of cyclists worldwide.

Our Mission

We believe that cycling is one of the most accessible, sustainable, and joyful forms of transportation and recreation. Our mission is to empower every rider — from first-time commuters to seasoned racers — with practical, honest, and data-driven content that helps them ride better, safer, and smarter.

Every article on this site is written by real cyclists who have actually used the gear, ridden the trails, and made the mistakes we write about. We don't publish anything we haven't personally tested or thoroughly researched.

Our Values

  • Honesty: We never recommend gear we wouldn't buy ourselves. Our reviews are based on real-world testing, not affiliate commissions.
  • Practicality: We focus on what actually works, not what's trendy. Our maintenance guides use tools you can afford, not $5,000 shop equipment.
  • Inclusivity: Cycling is for everyone. We cover content for all skill levels, budgets, and riding styles.
  • Accuracy: We cite verifiable sources, reference manufacturer specifications, and update our content when new information becomes available.

How We Fund Our Work

The World of Bicycles is funded through Google AdSense advertising. The ads you see on our pages help us cover hosting costs, equipment for testing, and the time it takes to research and write quality content. We maintain a strict separation between our editorial content and advertising — ads never influence what we write or recommend.

We also participate in the Amazon Associates program and other affiliate programs. When you purchase through our links, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. Products are never recommended based on commission rates.

Our Reach

Since launching in 2020, The World of Bicycles has published over 200 articles and serves over 150,000 monthly readers. Our content has been cited by cycling publications including Bicycling Magazine, Cycling Weekly, and BikeRadar. We've been featured in local cycling advocacy campaigns and have partnered with community bike shops across North America for real-world testing.
